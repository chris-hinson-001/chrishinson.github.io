About Us

Founded in Manchester 2013 by our Chief Product Officer, Noah Arkus RVN. NPC was born with the belief that veterinary healthcare should be made accessible to everyone. Over the past 5 years we have grown to 9 clinics across the UK with our latest clinic opening in Portsmouth 2018. We do things differently and challenge the status quo when it comes to pet health. We believe in making a positive change to the pet health industry by offering our patients exceptional care and support, 2 days a week with fair and value pricing. Our team are available during Mondays and Fridays with walk-in and appointments to suit your schedule.
